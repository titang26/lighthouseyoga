Hello Beautiful Souls,  
i’m Aurélie, A Yoga Teacher Originally From Mauritius. After Moving To Australia And Working In Corporate Banking, Yoga Became My Remedy For Stress, Transforming My Life. I Completed My Teacher Training At Byron Yoga Centre And Left The Corporate World In 2017 To Teach Full-time. Inspired By Cape Byron Lighthouse And Its Cultural Significance, I Founded Lighthouse yoga With Aurélie To Guide Others On Their  Journey Of Healing, Connection, And Community.
